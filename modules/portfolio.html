Python - Guess the Song app

This program is a Tkinter GUI-based song guessing game. It uses the pygame library to play music and displays songs' title and artist, in which users guess the correct song title by entering the answer into an entry box. The score is calculated and displayed to the user. The game continues with different random songs until the user makes two incorrect answers.
